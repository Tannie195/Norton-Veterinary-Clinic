Frequently Asked Questions

Below is a compiled list of our frequently asked questions and their answers what you should do next if a problem arises.

1. What vaccinations does my pet need

Core vaccinations like rabies and additional ones depending on age,breed and lifestlye.We will guide you based on their health and environment

2. How often should l bring my pet for a vaccination

At least once a year for healthy pets,senior ,puppies or sick animals may require more frequent visits

3. Do you offer home and farm visits

Yes we do,we also offer animal consultancy.dog breeding,parasite prevention services

4. Do you do routine surgeries like spaying and neutering

Yes we do under safe,hygienic conditions with proper aftercare

5. How do l book an appointment?

You can call or message us on whatsapp.We will confirm your time and service
