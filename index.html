Discover Who We Are

After Dr Priviledge Madzitire finished his prestigious degree in Veterinary Science at the University of Zimbabwe, he decided to start his own practice with the aim of providing the best care possible to ensure all animals are in good health and well catered for. We believe in Integrative Care For All Animals, bridging tradition and innovation.We are passionate about what we do, we strive for the best and believe in teamwork.
