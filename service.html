Our Services

Services That We Offer At Our Clinic

Animal Consultancy

Unlock the secrets and expert advice to optimal health and happiness so as to ensure your fury friends thrive remarkably!Whether you are a pet or livestock owner,we guide you to make the best decisions for your animals health,behaviour and nutrition

Home and Farm Visits

Convenience meets compassion!Can't come to us?We'll come to you.Our veterinary professionals deliver quality care and services directly at your doorstep,tending to animals in their own space making it convenient,stress-free and timely

Vaccinations

Keep your pets protected and healthy with our comprehensive vaccination services designed to keep tails wagging and spirits high.We ensure they stay healthy,safe and up to date with all the required and neccessary shots


Parasite Prevention

Say goodbye to fleas,ticks,worms!We offer effective and affordable parasite control to keep your animals healthy and itch-free all year round be it grooming,dipping or administering preventative medications ie deworming tablets

Routine Surgery

From surgical procedures like spaying and neutering to minor corrective provcedures ,our skilled team handles surgeries with precision,safety and care ,prioritising yor pets cormfort and recovery every step of the way

Dog Breeding

Discover the joy of breeding!We support responcible and healthy dog breeding.From heat tracking to pregnancy care,we ensure both pups and parent get the best start.We provide guidance on puppy socialisation and training.


StockFeeds

Nutritious stockfeed that keeps your livestock strong,healthy,and productive

Agrochemicals

Power your crops with trusted agrochemicals for healthier yields,season after season

Accesories
